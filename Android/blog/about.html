ABOUT


This is a very simple deep learning application which can detect up to 6 types of diseases after studying sample medical images. The diseases included in this application are — Malaria , Brain Tumor, Optical Coherence Tomography, Diabetes Retinopathy, Breast Cancer and Pneumonia using chest x-ray images. This is a beta version developed under the MIT open source license. Please feel free to re-use and re-distribute the code. Feel free to give feedback and suggestions. Your feedback will help us learn more and improve our application Of all the models, the malaria detection has the largest accuracy of 97%, the brain tumor has the least accuracy of 65%. This is largely due to data constraints. I had very limited data samples for my brain tumor models. Will try to improve upon the models in future updates. The application is not fully optimized to run at the fastest speed and handle multiple client requests using socket programming. If anyone has an idea on these topics, feel free to use them. I will try it in the future updates.
